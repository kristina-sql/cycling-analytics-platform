# Privacy Policy – Public Cycling Stats

**Last updated:** 27 January 2026

Public Cycling Stats is a web application that allows athletes to connect their Strava account to view summaries and statistics of **public cycling activities**.

---

## Data We Access
With the athlete’s explicit authorization, the app accesses the following data from Strava:
- Public athlete profile information
- **Public activities of type “Ride” (cycling)**

The app requests only the **read** permission from Strava.

---

## Data We Do Not Access
The app does **not** access:
- Private activities
- Followers-only activities
- Health or biometric data
- Messages, comments, or social interactions
- Any data unrelated to cycling activities

---

## How We Use the Data
The accessed data is used solely to:
- Display summaries and statistics of public cycling activities
- Provide basic insights such as distance, time, elevation, and activity dates

The data is not used for advertising and is not shared with third parties.

---

## Data Storage
Access tokens are stored securely and are used only to retrieve Strava data for the authorized athlete. Data is processed only for the functionality of the app.

---

## Revoking Access
Athletes can revoke access to the app at any time through their Strava account settings:  
https://www.strava.com/settings/apps

Once access is revoked, the app can no longer retrieve data from Strava.

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
